# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Financial analysts, researchers, and individuals looking for an AI-native financial analysis platform. They need quick, reliable data ingestion, semantic search over financial documents, and agent-driven analysis.

## Product Purpose
FinLab is an AI-native financial analysis and research laboratory. It leverages a modern Next.js frontend and a FastAPI backend with Qdrant vector database integration to ingest financial data, search through it semantically, and provide AI-driven insights to users. 

## Brand Personality
Professional, analytical, precise, modern, and reliable. It should feel like a premium, state-of-the-art financial terminal rather than a toy.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, overwhelming dashboards typical of legacy financial software.
- Black-box AI tools that don't let users trace back to the original financial data.
- Slow, unresponsive data tables.

## Design Principles
1. **Data Density without Clutter**: Display rich financial information clearly, using white space and typography to guide the eye.
2. **Speed and Reliability**: Financial data needs to be fast. UI interactions should feel instantaneous.
3. **Analytical Clarity**: Charts, metrics, and insights must be unambiguous and easy to read at a glance.
4. **Agentic Power**: Highlight AI analysis subtly but powerfully, integrating it naturally into the research workflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for data tables and charts.
- Clear typographic hierarchy for scanning dense financial reports.
